Objectives of Logistics

Keep in mind! ðŸ’­


1 To ensure the efficient and effective flow of goods, services, and information from the point of origin to the point of consumption, in order to meet customer requirements and support business operations.
2 To minimize costs associated with logistics activities, such as transportation, storage, handling, and inventory, while maximizing the value received by customers.
3 To provide reliable and timely delivery of products, thereby enhancing customer satisfaction and building customer loyalty.
4 To manage risk associated with logistics activities, such as the risk of damage to goods in transit, the risk of stockouts or excess inventory, and the risk of supply chain disruptions.
5 To achieve a high level of coordination and collaboration among all stakeholders in the supply chain, including suppliers, manufacturers, distributors, retailers, and customers.
6 To support sustainability initiatives, such as reducing carbon emissions from transportation, minimizing waste through efficient use of resources, and using environmentally friendly practices in all logistics activities.
